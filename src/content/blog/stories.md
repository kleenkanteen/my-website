I was laying in bed recently and thinking how many different situations I've been in. I can stay up all night replaying my memories. 

Specifically the last 11 years (I'm 25): high school, uni (4 years + 1 year co-op), and 2 years post grad of me doing startups.

It includes the people I met or worked with, the situations I've been in, the mistakes I made and the successes I netted. The things I've seen others do, the people who'veo been kind to me, the people who've been unkind to me.

As I write this at 2:19am, I'm in an uncertain place in my life.

I am optimistic and making moves to embark on a pursuit (startup or job), but I am also nervous about what happens. 

There are currently 2 things that I am waiting on to play out; one in the next week and the other over a few weeks. They can coexist but only for a short while. Don't even know if either will pan out.

I wish I could write an article here about each interesting experience I had, in order to share them with the world. Then others can learn from my unique perspective that shaped me.

And I hope others do the same in return. What an interesting and infinite amount of stories it would be...
